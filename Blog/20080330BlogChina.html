China

03/30/2008

Location: China- Beijing, Xi’an, Tsedang, Gyantse, Sandouping, Lanzhou, Jiayuguan, Dunhuang, Turpan, Kashgar, Urumqi, Shanghai, Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Hong Kong Travelers: 22; after abroad: 1 Duration: 3 months Note: We were rerouted because of protests in Tibet, the earth quake in Chengdu, and we went to Xinjiang where there were minority protests with the pre-Olympic build-up Itinerary Highlights: Chinese Language Classes; Helping English-speaking Olympic Volunteers prepare; Watching Pre-Olympic US-China Women basketball game; Bargaining in local markets; 18-Variety Dumpling Feast; Cruise Tour of Three Gorges Dam; Overnight train to Shanghai; Terra Cotta Soldiers in Xi'an; Visiting Great Wall in 3 different spots; Riding camels and jeeps at Dune in Gansu Province; Dancing in Turpan; Camping at Lamma Island, Hong Kong; Staying with host family in Guangzhou
