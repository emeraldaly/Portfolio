Calling London

04/01/2012

A few weeks ago on my commute to work I saw a woman walking down the street on a phone. Not a wanna be "CIA blue tooth" phone and not a "brick smooshed into her face" smartphone. An actual full handled red phone. I had to have one. Not having a particularly rebellious childhood, I see my red phone as the mohawk/tattoo/attention garnering accessory that I never wore. It has been fun walking around talking and noticing the unusual amount of attention it generates. When I went to London I knew that I needed to bring it. [gallery columns="2"] Today it is hung up in my room. I learned that in addition to not fitting in my purse, it short circuits my phone so that I can not hear out of the handset unless I reboot the phone. Fun, but not practical.
